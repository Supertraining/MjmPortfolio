Heidegger sostiene que el hombre es 'un ser para la muerte'. La mayoría se pasa la vida intentando negarlo y se entrega  a lo que él llamó una 'existencia inauténtica'... el precio que se por no saber nada de la propia muerte? ...vive un mundo que otros crean por él. Lee lo que dicen que hay que leer, vota a quien le indican que hay que votar y opina lo que debe opinarse. ...El que cree saber y no sabe, cree elegir y no elige. El que renuncia a la pasión del misterio y acata lo que otros han pensado por él.

Heidegger maintains that man is 'a being for death'. Most spend their lives trying to deny it and indulge in what he called an 'inauthentic existence'... the price to be paid for not knowing anything about their own death? ...lives a world that others create for him. Read what they say you have to read, vote for who they tell you to vote for and give your opinion on what should be said. ...He who thinks he knows and doesn't know, thinks he chooses and doesn't choose. He who renounces the passion for mystery and abides by what others have thought for him.
